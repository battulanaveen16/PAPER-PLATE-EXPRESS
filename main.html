Frequently Asked Questions

What's the minimum order for delivery?

Our minimum order varies by delivery zone. For Zone A (Vellore City, Katpadi, etc.), the minimum order is ₹300. For Zone B (Bagayam, Thorapadi, etc.), it's ₹400. For Zone C (Ranipet, Arcot, etc.), it's ₹500.

How fast can I get my order delivered?

For Zones A, B, and C, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM.

Do you offer bulk order discounts?

Yes, we provide special pricing for bulk orders. Please contact us directly for more information on volume discounts.
